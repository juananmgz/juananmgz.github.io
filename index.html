Who am I?

Welcome! I'm Juanan, a computer engineer specialized in Web Development & Agile Methodologies. My preferences are interface design and user experience (UI & UX) based on Human-Computer Interaction (HCP). My goal is to make intuitive and user-friendly websites and web applications.

I consider myself a very responsible and committed person, with a high level of perfectionism and detail. I want everything I do to have the best possible result within my knowledge and experience.
